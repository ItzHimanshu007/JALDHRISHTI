User Guide

Operational guide for the Jal Drishti Flood Intelligence Dashboard

Quick Start

01

Select Region

Choose a target village from the top-left dropdown to load local terrain and ML metrics.

02

Activate Simulation

Click the "Play" button on the bottom bar to watch flood progression over a 24-hour cycle.

03

Inspect Layers

Toggle 3D Buildings, Flow Vectors, or Risk Grid using the floating command panel on the right.

04

Analyze Cells

Click any point on the map to view a live telemetry "Cell Scan" of local water depth and risk.

Understanding Risk Intelligence

Extreme / High Risk

Calculated score > 0.8. Typically signifies water depth exceeding 500mm in flat or low-lying areas. Urgent monitoring required.

Medium Risk

Score 0.6 ‚Äì 0.8. Indicates 200‚Äì500mm water depth. Potential for localized property damage and road closures.

Low Risk

Score < 0.6. Minimal water accumulation (< 200mm) or steep terrain where water sheds rapidly after rainfall.

System Reliability

Decision Support Tool:

This dashboard is designed to aid disaster management authorities in visualizing potential inundation scenarios. It is optimized for rainfall-driven flash floods and pluvial events.

Technical Limitations:

The model assumes a uniform rainfall distribution across the village area. It does not dynamically account for sub-surface drainage systems or local infrastructure blockage.
